## Eligibility Requirements

Confirm your program meets these criteria before applying.

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Program Type | Youth-focused enrichment: sports, music, arts, STEM, camps, or after-school activities |
| Location | Operating in one of our 10 supported states: CA, TX, FL, NY, IL, PA, OH, GA, NC, MI |
| Scale Potential | Minimum `50` participants per session; potential to double enrollment within 2 years |
| Non-Profit Status | 501(c)(3) preferred; for-profits with strong social impact mission considered |
| Track Record | At least `1` year of operation with positive feedback from families |

<Callout kind="tip">
  Unsure about eligibility? Use our quick assessment tool at `/assessment` or contact us directly.
</Callout>

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What counts as youth enrichment?">
    Programs serving kids ages `5-18` in non-academic activities that build skills, confidence, and community. Examples include soccer leagues, guitar lessons, robotics clubs, and day camps.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Can for-profits apply?">
    Yes, if your mission prioritizes impact over profit and you demonstrate community benefits.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Application Process

Follow these steps to submit your partnership application.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Your Materials" icon="file-text">
    Gather program details: mission statement, participant numbers, financials, and growth plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Application" icon="upload">
    Complete the online form with your documents. Expect `2-3` business days for initial review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule Interview" icon="calendar">
    If shortlisted, book a `30-minute` video call to discuss your goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Offer" icon="check-circle">
    Approved partners sign agreements and kick off onboarding.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Initial Contact Methods

Reach out using your preferred channel. Response times vary.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Online Form" icon="form">
    Fill out the contact form for fastest processing (`24` hours).
    
    1. Visit `/contact`
    2. Select "Partnership Inquiry"
    3. Provide program name, location, and `50`-word summary
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">
    Send details to `partners@rocketyouth.org`.
    
    Include:
    
    - Program name and website
    - Annual participant count
    - Funding needs (`e.g., $50,000` for facility upgrade)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Phone" icon="phone">
    Call `1-800-ROCKET-Y` (`Mon-Fri, 9AM-5PM ET`).
    
    Ask for the Partnerships team and reference "New Partner Inquiry".
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Onboarding Timeline

Expect this timeline after approval.

| Phase | Duration | Milestones |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Agreement Signing | `1` week | Review and sign partnership agreement |
| Due Diligence | `2` weeks | Financial audit and reference checks |
| Funding Disbursement | `1` week | Receive initial capital |
| Operational Kickoff | `4` weeks | Training sessions and support plan launch |
| Full Partnership | `8` weeks total | Go-live with scaled programs |

<Callout kind="success">
  Over `90%` of partners launch within `8` weeks. Track progress via your partner dashboard.
</Callout>
