## Overview

Rocket Youth partners with youth enrichment and after-school programs to help them scale. You provide sports, music, camps, or similar services, and we offer capital investments plus operational support. Review these common questions to understand eligibility, applications, and next steps.

<Callout kind="info">
Rocket Youth operates in 10 states and has supported over `65,000` families. Check your location matches our coverage area before applying.
</Callout>

## Partnership Eligibility

Determine if your program qualifies for Rocket Youth support.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Basic Requirements" icon="check-circle">
    Serve youth ages `5-18` in after-school or enrichment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Program Focus" icon="activity">
    Sports, music, arts, camps, or STEM activities.
  </Card>
</Columns>

| Criterion          | Details                                      |
|--------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Location           | `10` states: CA, TX, NY, FL, IL, PA, OH, GA, NC, MI |
| Annual Revenue     | `> $100,000`                                 |
| Families Served    | `> 50` per year                              |
| Non-Profit Status  | Preferred, but for-profits considered        |

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What if my program is new?" default-open="true">
    New programs with strong leadership and initial traction qualify. Submit pilot data showing `20+` families engaged.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Do religious programs qualify?">
    Yes, if open to all families regardless of faith.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Application Tips

Follow these steps to submit a strong application.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Documents" icon="file-text">
    Gather financials, program metrics, and impact reports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Form" icon="edit-3">
    Fill out our online form with details on `families served`, revenue, and growth plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit & Follow Up" icon="send">
    Email `partnerships@rocketyouth.org` for status updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Sports Programs" icon="activity">
    Highlight participation rates and safety records. Example metric: `"500+ athlete hours per season"`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Music & Arts" icon="music">
    Share performance attendance and retention stats. Example: `"80%` return rate year-over-year"`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Camps" icon="sun">
    Provide enrollment numbers and parent feedback. Target: `"200+ campers per summer"`.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
Tailor your application to show scalability. Quantify impact with numbers like families served or revenue growth.
</Callout>

- Capital: Up to `$500,000` for expansion.

- Timeline: Funding within `90` days of approval.
