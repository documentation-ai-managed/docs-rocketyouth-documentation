## Our Mission

Rocket Youth partners with youth enrichment and after-school programs to help them scale their impact. You benefit from our capital investments and operational support, enabling your organization to reach more families through sports, music, camps, and similar initiatives. We amplify the positive effects of these vital programs.

<Callout kind="success" collapsed="false">
  Rocket Youth has served over `65,000` families across `10` states.
</Callout>
